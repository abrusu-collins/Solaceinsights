What powers autonomous vehicles?

It seems magical just to think about vehicles moving on the road with no human in the driverâ€™s seat. Unfortunately, it would not be a magical wand that sparked it in motion. Autonomous vehicles are vehicles capable of sensing its environment by the combination of sensors and software that enables the vehicle to have the control it needs to drive. Autonomous vehicles operate in six levels which are standards accepted by the U.S Department of Transportation. These can further be categorized into two groups: human monitoring the driving environment and the automated system monitoring the driving environment. In the first group,humans have a part to play in the driving process whereas in the second group, no human intervention is required for the vehicle to carry out its actions.

A picture depicting how autonomous vehicles are able to sense their environment.

Autonomous vehicle systems are able to create internal maps of their driving environments by use of a number of sensors like the radar and high-powered cameras which are then processed by the system to design plots of path the vehicle should take. Other elements such as actuators help in controlling the steering and the braking and also computer algorithms,predictive modelling among others help the vehicle in the detection of objects based on their differences. Do autonomous vehicles promise to be the future of transportation?

Autonomous vehicles are fascinating and overwhelming to see in action but could there be pitfalls associated with them? Will further testing of these cars lead to them being totally safe and approved for consumption by the public? There are certainly doubts about self driving cars being in full gear in the coming years. This may be due to certain pitfalls like it being vulnerable to hackers who can get access to the software and control the vehicle any how they want. Another could be about computer malfunctions which are likely to occur since these vehicles work with so much technology and a glitch at a certain part may cause harm to the whole system. With how roads and cities differ,will the self driving vehicle be able to deal with the issue of unfamiliar roads? Talk of accidents that are likely to happen if a self driving car fails to make a good detection of a road since it is not familiar with it.
